About Us

The main aim of our project is to work towards enhancement of userâ€™s experience by improving the lifestyle of people who have to sit in front of systems for prolonged hours which can in turn be the root cause of various alarming problems like back pain, spinal dysfunction, joint degeneration, rounded shoulders. We have thus implemented a system using a post estimation model based on computer vision, that will monitor the sitting posture of the user continuously via a live video feed. At any point of time if the posture of the neck, shoulders or arms is inappropriate in a way that it can cause physical tension to the user, the user will get notified about the same. This will thus help the user to maintain a good posture and improve their lifestyle. This project was motivated by a desire to help office workers change their sedentary behavior because a prolonged sedentary posture increases the risks of developing musculoskeletal injuries and chronic diseases, thus threatening their physical and psychological well-being.
